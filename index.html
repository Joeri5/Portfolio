about

about me.

Hi, I am Joeri Schenk. I am 16 years old and I'm from the Netherlands. I'm currently studying Software Development, in Amsterdam. I have around 5 years of experience in Front End web Development and Design. I started to make websites at the age of 11. I started to learn simple HTML and CSS. I made a site for fathers day and realized I liked to make websites and designs. At the age of 14 I started to take a class where I did a lot of experience with HTML, CSS, JavaFX and JavaScript. I got an assignment to make a website, it was a simple HTML and CSS site.

Since then, I got focussed to learn the difficult elements of HTML, started to improve my CSS skills, started to learn more about JavaScript and messed around with SCSS. Nowadays I'm specialized in Design and in Front End Web Development. I have gained a lot of experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and SCSS. I also have experience in working with Python, Java, ReactJS, NextJS and ThreeJS. I make designs in Figma and Adobe XD. I also have experience in working with Shopify and WordPress. I like to give all my projects a twist of my own, and learn new things.
